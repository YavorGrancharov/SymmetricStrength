Chronology of the training process


1
First session
Initial test for determining functional mobility (FMT) of the upper and lower half of the body. Introduction on basic movements and technical requirements for their proper and safe execution. Body control and control of the external objects. Understanding the method of training, its purpose, as well as some basic concepts and their meaning in the context of the free weight training.
Second session
Measuring subcutaneous fat percentage by caliperometry method. Determining the limits of the ideal active weight depending on sex, height and bone structure. Identifying specific targets according to results of the above measurements. Setting maximum and minimum daily calorie intake. Repeat the movements learned at the first training session.
2
3
Fourth session
Learning a new movement. Base weights of the moves learned so far have been established. You are already aware of what power is, how it develops, and why
